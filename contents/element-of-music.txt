Following poetry,
decide on
the tempo,
the beat,
the metre.

Then,
timbral threads,
some texture,
counterpoint the harmony,
ornament the solo motif,
throwup under the bridge,
and repeat it with the hook,
not forgetting, that silence is the count of the heart.
