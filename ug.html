UG Krishnamurthi

"Humanity is as fast as fear as slow as the slowest philosopher" "What is it to me ?" The position of UG is that of Radical Constructivism in Modern Day terms. It doesn't matter how you put it because Knowledge is continuously transformed according to the Subject, like air and industry. Abridged Summary ================ Ontological Reality can never be discovered. Realities are Infinite. Knowledge is not passively received but actively built. Knowledge is shared. Knowledge serves in organizing an experimental world. Power plays a role in the production of Knowledge. Knowledge-s compete. Knowledge useful for the survival of a subject / organization, survives. "Successful" Knowledge is defined as equilibrium / imitation of sorts. Equilibrium does not exist, only straight lines, or whatever we image. There are an infinite number of unique experiences. Knowledge used for creation can also be used for destruction by simple dialectic. Knowledge building is continuous till death. Knowledge can't be separated from the knower. Knowledge is never communicated, it's representation is. Representations must allow uncertainty to capture "bits" of Reality. Knowledge lives as long as memory is reinforced, cruelly. Therapy as re framing. The same for this.
